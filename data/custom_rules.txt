# Custom Review Rules — OPO Finance Support

These rules describe how OPO broker works. Follow them exactly when scoring agents.

---

## COMMISSION RULE

At OPO broker, commission is deducted ONLY when a position is OPENED.
When a position is CLOSED, there is NO commission.

This applies to ALL account types including ECN.

So for any trade:
- Entry (open) → commission deducted once
- Exit (close) → no commission, zero

An agent who tells the customer "commission is only charged when you open the position"
or "there is no commission when closing" is giving a 100% correct answer.

Do NOT penalize the agent for this explanation.
Do NOT suggest the agent's answer is wrong or misleading.
The agent's answer IS the correct OPO policy.
