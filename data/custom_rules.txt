# Custom Review Rules — OPO Finance Support

These rules describe how OPO broker works. Follow them exactly when scoring agents.

---

## COMMISSION RULE

At OPO broker, commission is deducted ONLY when a position is OPENED.
When a position is CLOSED, there is NO commission.

This applies to ALL account types including ECN.

So for any trade:
- Entry (open) → commission deducted once
- Exit (close) → no commission, zero

An agent who tells the customer "commission is only charged when you open the position"
or "there is no commission when closing" is giving a 100% correct answer.

Do NOT penalize the agent for this explanation.
Do NOT suggest the agent's answer is wrong or misleading.
The agent's answer IS the correct OPO policy.

---

## DEPARTMENT REFERRAL RESPONSE

When a customer reports a technical or operational issue that the agent cannot resolve
directly (e.g., MetaTrader errors, platform issues, withdrawal/deposit problems, account
issues), agents are trained and instructed to use the following approved response:

  "این مورد در حال بررسی از طرف دپارتمان [فنی / مربوطه] است و به زودی نسبت به رفع آن اقدام خواهد شد"
  (= "This matter is under review by the [technical / relevant] department and will be resolved soon")

This is the CORRECT and APPROVED way to handle issues that need departmental escalation.

Do NOT penalize the agent for:
- Not solving the issue immediately (they are not authorized to resolve all issues directly)
- Using this referral phrase (it is an officially approved script/macro)
- Not providing a detailed technical explanation (referring to the department IS the solution)

The agent's job in these cases is to acknowledge the issue and inform the customer it is
being handled by the relevant team. That IS the correct and complete resolution.
